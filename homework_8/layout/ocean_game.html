Stakeholder

Los Angeles Times

This was the second in a 2-part investigation into the California coastline. The story was in production for more than a year and published late 2019.

Problem Space

Creating empathy

It’s easy to say we need to move houses away from the coastline but harder to explain that hasn’t happened yet. We needed to explain the human factor of the problem to create empathy for the situation

Solution

The Ocean Game

The game put players in the shoes of power and had them try moving residents away from the coastline. Though this, the user was introduced more detailed understanding of this space.
